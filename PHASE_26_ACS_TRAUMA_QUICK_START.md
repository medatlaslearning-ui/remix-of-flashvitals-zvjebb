## üìä UNDERSTANDING RECOMMENDATIONS

### Recommendation Levels

**Level 1 (Strong):**
- High quality evidence
- Strong recommendation
- Should be followed in most cases

**Level 2 (Moderate):**
- Moderate quality evidence
- Moderate recommendation
- Consider patient-specific factors

**Level 3 (Weak):**
- Low quality evidence or expert opinion
- Weak recommendation
- Use clinical judgment

### Quality of Evidence

**Quality A:** High quality evidence from randomized controlled trials

**Quality B:** Moderate quality evidence from observational studies

**Quality C:** Low quality evidence or expert opinion
