## üìä Understanding KDIGO Recommendations

### Recommendation Strength
- **Grade 1 (Strong):** "We recommend..."
  - Benefits clearly outweigh risks
  - Most patients should receive this intervention
  
- **Grade 2 (Weak/Conditional):** "We suggest..."
  - Benefits and risks closely balanced
  - Different choices appropriate for different patients

### Quality of Evidence
- **A (High):** High confidence in the estimate
- **B (Moderate):** Moderate confidence in the estimate
- **C (Low):** Limited confidence in the estimate
- **D (Very Low):** Very little confidence in the estimate
